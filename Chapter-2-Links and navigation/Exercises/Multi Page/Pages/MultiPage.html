Plot

The plot follows Pete "Maverick" Mitchell, a young naval aviator who aspires to be the top pilot in the Navy's elite fighter weapons school known as TOPGUN. Maverick's reckless flying and arrogant attitude put him at odds with the other pilots, particularly his rival, Iceman. Along the way, Maverick falls for Charlie, an astrophysics instructor, and must confront his own demons to become the best fighter pilot he can be.
